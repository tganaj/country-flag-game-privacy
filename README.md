# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how "Guess the Flag" ("the App", "we", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Player name:** When joining a team challenge, you enter a display name. This name is stored temporarily on our servers for the duration of the challenge and is visible to other participants.
- **Challenge data:** When creating or joining team challenges, quiz scores, completion times, and answers are stored temporarily to enable the multiplayer experience.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device camera:** The App requests camera access solely to scan QR codes for joining team challenges. Camera data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted or stored.

### Third-Party Services

#### Google AdMob (Free version only)
The free version of the App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect and process certain information including:
- Device identifiers (advertising ID)
- IP address
- General location (country/region level)
- App usage data

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

#### Firebase Realtime Database
Team challenge data (challenge codes, participant names, scores, and timestamps) is stored on Firebase Realtime Database to enable multiplayer functionality. This data is:
- Stored temporarily for the duration of the challenge
- Not linked to any personal account or identifier
- Not used for analytics or advertising purposes

For more information, see [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

## Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not require account creation or login
- We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or real names
- We do not collect precise location data
- We do not track users across other apps or websites
- We do not sell any user data to third parties

## Data Storage
- **Quiz scores and Hall of Fame:** Stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.
- **App preferences** (language, skin, music settings): Stored locally on your device only.
- **Team challenge data:** Stored on Firebase servers in the EU (europe-west1 region) and retained only for the duration of the challenge.

## Children's Privacy
The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The team challenge feature requires only a display name (which can be any nickname) and does not collect any other personal information.

## Your Rights
- You can delete your local data (scores, preferences) by clearing the app data or uninstalling the App.
- You can reset your advertising ID through your device settings.
- You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
